About Pheliridge

Our Story

Pheliridge is a Non-Profit Company established in 2022 with a clear mission: decisive action for community transformation. Our name is derived from two powerful sources - Phelindaba (which means "the place of Action") and Atteridgeville (ridge). This reflects our commitment: all talk must end and action begins.

We are strategically positioned in West Pretoria, Atteridgeville - an area facing significant social and economic challenges including high unemployment, shortage of skills, limited facilities, and demotivated youth. As community members who grew up facing these challenges, we committed ourselves to be the change our community needs.

Our Mission

We address the challenges of youth unemployment in the community through skills development that improves employability and assists youth to consider self-employment as an option between employment or as their main career.

Our Vision

To create a thriving community where every young person has the skills, confidence, and opportunities to build a successful future, free from poverty and crime.

Our Commitment & Objectives

High-Performing Institution

To become a place where students love to learn, educators inspire, and the community trusts the system.

Economic Growth

To add value in growth areas and entities within the economic industry through skill development.

Community Voice

To be the voice of organizations and communities, showcasing how arts and cultural strategies deepen engagement.

Social Fabric

To strengthen communities and achieve key goals in housing, health, and neighborhood development.
